Top 5 Eco-Friendly Stadiums

Modern stadiums are not just about sports—they're also about sustainability. Here are 5 eco-friendly stadiums that are leading the way in green innovation and redefining what it means to be sustainable.

Top 5 Eco-Friendly Stadiums

  • 1. Mercedes-Benz Stadium (USA): LEED Platinum certified, featuring rainwater recycling and solar panels.
  • 2. Allianz Arena (Germany): Known for energy-efficient LED lighting and advanced waste management systems.
  • 3. Forest Green Rovers Stadium (UK): Made almost entirely of wood, it’s powered by renewable energy.
  • 4. Estádio Mineirão (Brazil): Incorporates solar panels and water reuse systems.
  • 5. Kaohsiung National Stadium (Taiwan): Powered entirely by solar energy, with a design that minimizes environmental impact.
